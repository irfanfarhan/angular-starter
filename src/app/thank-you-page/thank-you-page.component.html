Thank you for participating in the survey. This survey is intended to identify the potential prevalence of Pompe at the population level as well as to identify potential individuals at high risk of Pompe. Please note that a positive response to one or more of these questions is not indicative of Pompe. No diagnosis can be made on the basis of any questionnaire. Please note that a confirmed diagnosis of Pompe requires a blood test and a genetic test. The main purpose of this survey is to sensitize people to this rare disorder. Since LOPD is a slowly progressive disorder, you can revisit the survey at a later time point or visit your doctor in the event of observing some or more of these symptoms. Early diagnosis can help in early intervention and thereby delay the onset of severe symptoms.

Based on the answers you have provided and your current age, the risk report will be emailed to you in the next 72 hours on the e-mail address provided by you.
